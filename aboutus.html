About Us

Love discussing business? We do too!

Consulting, though a fascinating field, has been a latent passion among students at DTU. The absence of a formal platform has scattered the business enthusiasts across the campus. Determined to change this landscape, the DTU Consulting Group was born.

People | Productivity | Profitability

At the core of our Society are three pillars: People, Productivity, and Profitability. We value the importance of building meaningful connections, fostering a productive environment, and understanding the dynamics that drive profitability in the business world.

At DCG, we are on a mission to cultivate a robust consulting culture within our college. We aim to be the rallying point for business enthusiasts, providing them with the formal structure and resources needed to develop their consulting skills. By doing so, we hope to empower our members with invaluable business sense that will prove beneficial in the long run.
