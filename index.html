Hi, Welcome to Social Pulse
by DCG

A new intiative to democratise more consulting related information

A dynamic hub for both seasoned consultants and aspiring professionals eager to delve into the intricate world of consultancy, opening the doors to a wealth of insights for enthusiasts at every level.

As a collaborative platform, we strive to foster an inclusive community where expertise is shared and cultivated. Here, you will find a treasure trove of resources, from meticulously compiled guesstimates to comprehensive casebooks, and thought-provoking interviews and blogs covering diverse facets of the consulting realm.

Our commitment is to provide a continuously evolving space, regularly updated with the latest industry trends and expert perspectives. Join us on this journey as we empower individuals to navigate the consultancy landscape, whether you're a seasoned professional seeking to deepen your knowledge or an aspiring consultant ready to embark on a transformative learning experience.

What You will Find Here

Blogs

Explore insightful blogs delving into consultancy intricacies, offering practical wisdom and visionary insights on industry trends, client communication, and methodologies.

Interviews

Gain valuable career insights through in-depth conversations with seasoned consultants and industry leaders, discovering their strategies and experiences in tackling complex business challenges.

Analysis

Navigate the evolving consulting landscape with comprehensive industry analyses, providing key insights into market trends, global events, and strategic implications for businesses.

Resources

Access a rich repository of resources, including guesstimate compilations, real-world casebooks, and templates, empowering you with essential tools to enhance your consulting toolkit and stay ahead in the field.
